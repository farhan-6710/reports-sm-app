## 🚀 How to Create Your First Organic Report

### Step 1: Open the Dashboard
Go to: **http://localhost:3000**

### Step 2: Click "Create Organic Report"
Look for the blue button in the top navigation bar

### Step 3: Choose Your Platform
- 🔴 **Instagram** - for Instagram Business/Creator accounts
- 🔵 **Facebook** - for Facebook Pages

### Step 4: Get Your Metrics from the App

#### For Instagram:
1. Open **Instagram app** on your phone
2. Go to your **Profile**
3. Tap **Professional dashboard** (or three lines menu)
4. Tap **Insights**
5. Look at **Overview** (last 30 days)

#### For Facebook:
1. Open **Facebook app** or website
2. Go to your **Page**
3. Click **Insights** (left menu)
4. Look at **Overview** (last 30 days)

### Step 5: Enter Your Numbers

The form is organized into 5 color-coded sections:

#### 📊 Account Stats (Blue)
- **Total Followers**: Your current follower count
- **Following**: How many you follow
- **Posts Published**: Posts in last 30 days

#### 👁️ Reach & Visibility (Green)
- **Total Impressions**: How many times content was shown
- **Accounts Reached**: Unique accounts who saw content
- **Profile Visits**: People who viewed your profile

#### 💬 Engagement (Yellow)
- **Total Likes**: All likes on posts
- **Total Comments**: Comments received
- **Shares**: Times content was shared
- **Saves**: Times content was saved

#### 🔗 Traffic & Actions (Light Blue)
- **Website Clicks**: Link clicks to your website
- **Email/Contact Clicks**: Contact button taps

#### 📸 Stories (Purple - Instagram Only)
- **Story Views**: Total story impressions
- **Story Replies**: DMs from stories

### Step 6: Generate Report
Click **"Generate Organic Report"** button at the bottom

### Step 7: View & Download
- See your report in the "Recent Reports" section
- Click **"Download PDF"** for professional report
- Click **"Download CSV"** for Excel analysis

## 🎨 What You Get

### Visual Reports Include:
1. **Summary Cards** with key metrics
2. **Bar Charts** comparing metrics
3. **Engagement Rate** calculation
4. **Growth indicators** (coming soon with multiple reports)
5. **Professional PDF** reports
6. **CSV data** for custom analysis

### Calculated Metrics:
- ✅ **Total Engagement** = Likes + Comments + Shares + Saves
- ✅ **Engagement Rate** = (Total Engagement / Followers) × 100
- ✅ **Average per Post** = Metrics / Posts Count

## 💡 Pro Tips

### 1. **Be Consistent**
- Enter data at the same time each month
- Use the same date range (e.g., always last 30 days)

### 2. **Take Screenshots**
- Screenshot your insights before entering
- Keep records for verification

### 3. **Track Trends**
- Create reports weekly or monthly
- Compare to see growth over time

### 4. **Client Reports**
- Add account name for clarity
- Download PDF for professional look
- Include date range in filename

### 5. **Data Accuracy**
- Double-check numbers before submitting
- Round decimals to whole numbers
- Use 0 if metric not available
